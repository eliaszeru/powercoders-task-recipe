Traditional Recipes

Doro Wot

Click here to see the step-by-step recipe

Steps:

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden.

2. Add garlic, ginger, and berbere spice, and cook for a few minutes.

3. Add chicken and cook until golden brown.

4. Add water, cover, and simmer until chicken is cooked through.

Dulet

Click here to see the step-by-step recipe

Steps:

1. Sauté onions and spices in oil.

2. Add finely chopped beef and cook until browned.

3. Stir in chili, salt, and pepper to taste.

4. Serve hot with injera or bread.

Modern Recipes

Pizza

Click here to see the step-by-step recipe

Steps:

1. Prepare the dough and let it rise for 1 hour.

2. Roll the dough into a pizza base and add sauce.

3. Add cheese, toppings, and bake in a preheated oven.

4. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.

Burger

Click here to see the step-by-step recipe

Steps:

1. Form patties from ground beef and season.

2. Grill patties until cooked through.

3. Toast burger buns and assemble with toppings.

4. Serve with fries or chips.
